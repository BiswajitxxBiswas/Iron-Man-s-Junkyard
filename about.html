Our Mission

Inspired by Tony Stark’s ability to turn scrap into life-saving technology, Iron Man's Junkyard Heroes is a revolutionary platform for scrap management. We empower customers and scrap dealers to make a real difference in recycling and sustainability by transforming waste into valuable resources.

Our mission is to make the scrap industry efficient, accessible, and impactful—saving resources, reducing waste, and maybe even sparking the creation of the next Iron Man suit!

Key Features

Basic Features

For Customers:

  • Easy Registration: Create an account and join the scrap revolution.
  • Request Pickups: Schedule scrap pickup by selecting a preferred date and time.
  • Feedback and Ratings: Share feedback and rate the scrap dealer after your scrap is sold.

For Scrap Dealers:

  • Dealer Access: Login with credentials provided by the admin and start accepting requests.
  • Billing System: Generate bills after purchasing scrap with itemized details for customer transparency.
  • Request History: Close completed requests for easy reference.

For Admins:

  • Administrative Access: Manage key aspects of the platform.
  • Pricing Control: Set up and adjust prices for various scrap items.

Advanced Features

For Customers:

  • Social Integration: Sign up quickly using Google or Facebook.
  • Social Sharing: Invite friends to the platform and spread the word on social media.
  • Flexible Scheduling: Provide multiple pickup date and time options.

For Scrap Dealers:

  • Location-Based Service: Set operational locations to receive relevant customer requests.
  • Availability Status: Manage your availability with an online/offline toggle.

For Admins:

  • Comprehensive Dashboard: Track daily purchases, request statuses, and customer feedback.
  • Notifications: Send timely alerts to customers and dealers.
